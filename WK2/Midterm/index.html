Luigi's Pizzeria Resturant

Luigi Romano

About Our Founder

Luigi Romano started a family business, that came from Italy of the age of 9 his parents always made him homemade pizza. It was his favorite pizza from any other pizzeria places around his neighborhood. Now that his parents passed away he decided to build this pizzeria store to make his parents happy by giving the people taste of real homemade pizza of the Romano family.

Our Competition

Their competition are Dominoes, Papa Johns, Pizza Hut, and any other local pizzeria. What makes them different from these type of pizzeria stores is that all ingredients are made fresh and received fresh, these slices are different because they are also low on calories.

Our Goal

Our goal is to have customers become regulars within the store and make them feel a part of the family business or a home. Our goal on the competitive side is to have a better-quality taste of pizza and better prices for their delicious food.
