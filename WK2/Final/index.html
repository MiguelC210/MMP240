About Our Founder

Alison Wick is a client from the Big Room Music Festivals. Big Room Music Festivals is an organization that provides you information on what to know when going to music festivals for your first time or if you will like to find more festivals to go to for you to have fun. They provide you with guide on how to survive these events and give exclusive discounts on tickets rather from paying the full price from these events.

Our Competition

Their competition is NightLife Inc which provides only reviews on these music festivals, Big Room Music Festivals not only provides reviews but also gives you helpful guide tips on how to survive or expect in these music festivals. Also, including discounts on tickets for these music festivals around the world.

Our Goal

Our goal is to target our audience at any age of 18+ and we will like to show them the colorful, joyous, popular, yearly, celebrated, and how important music festivals are to us in the EDM music industry.
